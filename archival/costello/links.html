The Csound Frontpage - has links to all things Csound, including the upcoming Csound Book.

Synthesizer Schematics - A small page of interesting analog synthesizer circuits I have on my site.

IBM Patent Site - contains full text/images of patents from 1974 to the present date. Very useful for uncovering synthesis techniques that have been obscured by history.

Geofex - R.G. Keen's site for guitar effects technical information. Contains links to schematics of many fine effects, plus technical articles on the inner workings of many effects. (Note: I contributed the Tychobrahe Octavia schematic, after reverse engineering one of these rare pedals.)

Electronotes - Information for ordering the Electronotes newsletter. Electronotes is one of the great treasure troves of technical information on music synthesis, both analog and digital. I own the complete set; not cheap, but one of the best investments I have made. Reading Electronotes issues from the early 1970's to today provides a comprehensive overview of the state of the art in analog synthesizer design, and how this transfers to digital synthesis.

SMCC - The School of Music Computer Center at the University of Washington.

CCRMA

synth-diy - The synth-diy mailing list. Probably the finest source of technical information on analog synthesis since Electronotes. List members are exceptionally knowledgeable in all things relating to the theory and construction of analog synthesis. Many listmembers have been working on analog synths since the 1970s; some actually designed synths for companies such as Moog. Exceptionally friendly and helpful people.

music-dsp - A mailing list which I belong to and contribute to. Subject matter is useage of DSP in all musical applications. Rather poor signal-to-noise ratio, but excellent information occasionally surfaces.

Harmony Central - Excellent source of information for all things related to musical technology. Nice lists of links; some useful source code for music DSP.
